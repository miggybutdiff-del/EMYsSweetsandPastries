About Us

Emy’s Sweets and Pastries is a local business based in San Fernando, Pampanga, offering a variety of freshly made sweets and baked goods. Known for its quality and consistency, the shop continues to provide delicious treats for everyday enjoyment.

Our History

Established in 2010, Emy’s Sweets and Pastries started as a small homegrown business in San Fernando, Pampanga. Through the years, it has steadily grown, building a reputation for reliable service
